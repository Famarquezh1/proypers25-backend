ARCHITECTURAL IMPROVEMENTS OVER V1
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

DETECTION TIMING:
  v1: Waited for 0.5% 5m move (LATE, loses initial momentum)
  v2: Detects 0.2% 1m + 0.3% 3m (EARLY, captures START of impulse)
  Impact: +2-3x earlier entry point, better edge capture

OVEREXTENSION PROTECTION:
  v1: No check for already-moved prices
  v2: Rejects if move3m ≥ 0.6% (stops chasing overextended moves)
  Impact: Prevents "chasing" high-risk setups

CONFIRMATION LAYER:
  v1: Execute immediately on detection
  v2: Wait 1 cycle minimum, revalidate before execution
  Impact: Reduces false positives, validates impulse persistence

VOLUME THRESHOLD:
  v1: ≥ 1.5x average
  v2: ≥ 1.3x average (slightly lower for earlier detection)
  Impact: Balances institutional interest with early detection

STRENGTH SCORING:
  v1: Multiple factors, complex weighting
  v2: Simplified 3-factor model (move, extension, volume)
  Impact: More predictable and easier to tune
